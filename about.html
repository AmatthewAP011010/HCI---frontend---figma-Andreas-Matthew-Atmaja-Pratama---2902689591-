About Good OLd' Bakery

Our story, mission, and the people behind the bread

Our History

Good OLd' Bakery was founded in 1985 by Andreas Matthew in his small kitchen. What started as a hobby of baking bread for neighbors turned into a beloved local institution. For 40 years, we've been using the same recipes passed down through generations, ensuring every bite tastes like home.

Today, we have grown into multiple locations while maintaining our commitment to quality and tradition. Each loaf of bread, each pastry, and each cake is still made with the same love and care that Andreas Matthew put into her very first batch.

Our Vision

To be the most beloved bakery brand that brings joy to every table across the nation, preserving traditional baking methods while embracing innovation.

Our Mission

  • To craft high-quality, delicious baked goods using only the finest ingredients
  • To preserve traditional recipes and baking techniques
  • To create a warm, welcoming atmosphere in every store
  • To give back to the communities we serve
  • To train and empower local bakers
